Scraping Hacker News

I use the Python library Beautiful Soup to scrape the news content on Hacker News.

The results show only the articles with more than 100 points of upvotes on the first page of Hacker News. I can run the app from the Command Line on my computer without going on Hacker News directly and can still browse the most popular articles that my app has filtered.
